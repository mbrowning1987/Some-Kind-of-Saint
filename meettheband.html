Meet, Some Kind of Saint

Some Kind of Saint is a 5 man band, made up of 5 very different personalities, and musical taste, meshed together into one band, that can rock. If you get the chance, come check them out. Event calander is updated regularly.

Corey Robinson- Main Vocals

Corey, has been with the band since 2008. Corey is a husband, and a father of 4. Corey's musical influences and inspirations include, The Backstreet Boy, Shakira, and Vanilla Ice. Corey enjoys listening to, a wide range of music.
